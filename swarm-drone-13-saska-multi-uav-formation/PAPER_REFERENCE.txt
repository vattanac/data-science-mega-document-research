SUMMARY:
--------
Saska's group developed a complete decentralized pipeline for GPS-denied
multi-UAV formation flight. Drones use onboard cameras with UV-pass filters
to detect UV-LED markers on neighboring drones, enabling relative localization
at 30+ Hz with ±0.3m accuracy. The system has been validated outdoors with
up to 10 UAVs flying autonomously in various formations (V, line, diamond,
circle) with no GPS, motion capture, or external infrastructure.
