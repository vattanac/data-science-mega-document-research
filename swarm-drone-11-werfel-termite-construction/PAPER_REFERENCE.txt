ABSTRACT (paraphrased):
-----------------------
The paper presents the TERMES system — a multi-agent construction system
inspired by mound-building termites. The system automatically generates
low-level behavioral rules for independent climbing robots that guarantee
the production of a user-specified structure. Robots use only local sensing
and coordinate their activity via the shared environment (stigmergy),
requiring no direct communication between agents. The approach was
demonstrated with three autonomous climbing robots in physical experiments.
